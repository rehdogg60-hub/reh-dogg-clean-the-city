Reh Dogg: Clean the City

Welcome to Reh Dogg: Clean the City, a fast-paced browser game where players help a heroic vigilante restore order to the streets. Tap quickly, earn points, and use special power-ups to clean up crime and chaos before time runs out.

Game Overview

In this game, you step into the role of Reh Dogg, a fearless city protector armed with a powerful loofah staff that blasts soap suds. With help from his loyal dogs Maggie and Chip, you must clean the city by tapping dirty trouble as it appears.

The faster you react, the higher your score.

How to Play
Tap or click dirty targets to clean them
Each successful tap earns points
Avoid tapping civilians or you will lose points
Collect power-ups to boost your score
Try to beat your best score before time runs out

Game duration: 60 seconds

Power-Ups

Maggie
Freezes all targets for a short time

Chip
Clears multiple targets instantly

Soap Bubble
Gives bonus points
